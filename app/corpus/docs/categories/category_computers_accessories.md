# Category: computers_accessories (informatica_acessorios)

## What it covers
Computers, computer peripherals, and computing accessories. Includes: 
laptops, desktops, monitors, keyboards, mice, hard drives, SSDs, 
networking gear, computer cables, printers, scanners.

## What it does NOT cover
- Phones and tablets (use `telephony`)
- General electronics like audio devices (use `electronics`)
- Software (not represented in the dataset as a separate category)

## Collision cluster
Sits with `electronics` and `telephony` in the tech cluster. The 
Portuguese token `informatica_acessorios` is more specific than 
`eletronicos` but the boundary is fuzzy when items have computing 
components but aren't computers (smart speakers, e-readers).

## Common analytics
English name: `computers_accessories`. Active category, often appears 
in tech-related revenue questions. The compound token form is what 
causes the category-token blurring documented in the architecture bible.

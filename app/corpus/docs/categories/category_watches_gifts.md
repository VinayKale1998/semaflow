# Category: watches_gifts (relogios_presentes)

## What it covers
Watches and items typically purchased as gifts. Includes: wristwatches, 
pocket watches, jewelry boxes, gift sets, decorative gifts, fashion 
accessories often given as gifts.

## What it does NOT cover
- General fashion accessories not gift-oriented (catalogued elsewhere 
  if present)
- Electronics like smartwatches when listed as tech (often 
  miscategorized into `electronics`)

## Collision cluster
Sits with `sports_leisure` and `toys` in the leisure cluster. The token 
combines two concepts (watches and gifts) that don't always overlap. 
The "gifts" portion makes the category absorb gift-oriented items from 
many other domains.

## Common analytics
English name: `watches_gifts`. Consistently a top-revenue category. 
Stage 1 baseline showed it second only to health_beauty at ~1.21M BRL. 
This is one of the most active categories on the platform.

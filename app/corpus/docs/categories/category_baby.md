# Category: baby (bebes)

## What it covers
Products specifically designed for infants and small children. Includes: 
diapers, baby shampoo and lotion, baby food accessories, baby clothing, 
strollers, car seats, cribs, baby toys for newborns.

## What it does NOT cover
- General toys for older children (use `toys`)
- General personal care products usable by adults (use `health_beauty`)
- Children's clothing for older kids (catalogued under children's 
  apparel categories not in this dataset)

## Collision cluster
Sits with `health_beauty` and `perfumery` in the personal-care cluster. 
The token `bebes` embeds near `beleza_saude` because baby products are 
a subset of personal care. Boundary is age: baby is birth to small 
child only.

## Common analytics
English name: `baby`. Moderate revenue category. Demographics question 
target: "what fraction of customers buy baby products" is the kind of 
question that needs this category isolated, not lumped with health_beauty.

# Category: toys (brinquedos)

## What it covers
Toys for children of all ages. Includes: dolls, action figures, 
building blocks, educational toys, plush toys, board games for 
children, toy vehicles, puzzles for kids.

## What it does NOT cover
- Baby-specific toys for infants (use `baby`)
- Adult board games or hobby items (use `sports_leisure`)
- Electronics-based toys with computing elements (sometimes in 
  `electronics` due to miscategorization)

## Collision cluster
Sits with `sports_leisure` and `watches_gifts` in the leisure cluster. 
The boundary with `baby` matters: baby toys belong in baby, child toys 
belong here.

## Common analytics
English name: `toys`. Mid-tier revenue. Seasonal peaks around holidays 
(December) and Children's Day in October.

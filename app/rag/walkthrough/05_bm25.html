Why stopword removal, and what BM25 still gets wrong. Boilerplate section titles like "What it does NOT cover" match query words such as what and does, flooding the results with chunks that lack the actual identifier. Dropping those words lets order_status drive scoring. But BM25 normalizes by length, so short policy chunks that mention the token once rank above the long fact_orders.md column definition that explains it in depth. BM25's job here is recall into the candidate pool, not the final answer.

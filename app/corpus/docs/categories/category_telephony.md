# Category: telephony (telefonia)

## What it covers
Phones and phone-related accessories. Includes: smartphones, feature 
phones, phone cases, screen protectors, phone chargers, headsets, 
earbuds intended for phone use.

## What it does NOT cover
- Computer peripherals (use `computers_accessories`)
- Audio equipment unrelated to phones (use `electronics`)
- Phone services or plans (not sold on the platform)

## Collision cluster
Sits with `electronics` and `computers_accessories` in the tech cluster. 
The token `telefonia` is the most specific of the three, but customer 
queries about "phone accessories" or "headphones" can match across all 
three depending on how the listing was created.

## Common analytics
English name: `telephony`. One of the more active categories. Phone 
accessories are high-volume, low-ticket items, so this category often 
leads in item count but not revenue.

# Category: electronics (eletronicos)

## What it covers
General consumer electronics that don't fall into a more specific 
subcategory. Includes: small appliances with electronic components, 
audio equipment, entertainment devices, electronic accessories that 
are not computer or phone specific.

## What it does NOT cover
- Computers, laptops, and computer peripherals (use `computers_accessories`)
- Phones and phone accessories (use `telephony`)
- Large home appliances like refrigerators (use `home_appliances` or 
  similar where present)

## Collision cluster
Sits with `computers_accessories` and `telephony` in the tech cluster. 
The token `eletronicos` is the broadest of the three and absorbs items 
that should arguably go in a more specific category. This is the 
single biggest source of category miscategorization in the dataset.

## Common analytics
English name: `electronics`. Mid-tier revenue. When answering "tech 
revenue," sum across all three: electronics + computers_accessories + 
telephony.

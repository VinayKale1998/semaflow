# Category: sports_leisure (esporte_lazer)

## What it covers
Sporting goods, exercise equipment, and recreational items. Includes: 
exercise equipment, sports apparel, outdoor gear, camping equipment, 
bicycles, sports accessories, board games and puzzles for adults.

## What it does NOT cover
- Children's toys (use `toys`)
- Watches and accessories worn (use `watches_gifts`)
- Athletic shoes when catalogued separately under footwear

## Collision cluster
Sits with `toys` and `watches_gifts` in the leisure cluster. The token 
`esporte_lazer` combines two concepts (sport and leisure), which causes 
overlap with adjacent recreational categories. Board games, for example, 
could plausibly be `toys` or `sports_leisure`.

## Common analytics
English name: `sports_leisure`. Active mid-revenue category. Often 
relevant for seasonal analytics (summer outdoor activity spikes).

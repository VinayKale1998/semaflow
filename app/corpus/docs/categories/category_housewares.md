# Category: housewares (utilidades_domesticas)

## What it covers
General-utility household items, especially for the kitchen and 
day-to-day living. Includes: cookware, kitchen utensils, food storage 
containers, cleaning supplies, small kitchen tools, dinnerware, 
glassware.

## What it does NOT cover
- Decorative items (use `furniture_decor`)
- Bedding or bath linens (use `home_comfort`)
- Major appliances (use a separate appliances category)

## Collision cluster
Sits with `furniture_decor` and `home_comfort` in the home cluster. 
`utilidades_domesticas` translates literally to "domestic utilities," 
which overlaps semantically with the other two home categories. The 
boundary is function: housewares is for using (cooking, eating, 
cleaning), not for decorating or sleeping on.

## Common analytics
English name: `housewares`. Mid-tier revenue, high SKU count. Often 
combined with `furniture_decor` for "home goods" analytics, but the 
two answer different questions.

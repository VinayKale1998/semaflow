# Category: furniture_decor (moveis_decoracao)

## What it covers
Furniture pieces and decorative objects for the home. Includes: chairs, 
tables, sofas, shelves, wall art, decorative vases, lamps, rugs, 
curtains, ornamental objects.

## What it does NOT cover
- Bedding, towels, and bath linens (use `home_comfort`)
- Kitchen and dining utensils (use `housewares`)
- Garden furniture (sometimes catalogued under separate garden 
  categories)

## Collision cluster
Sits with `home_comfort` (cama_mesa_banho) and `housewares` 
(utilidades_domesticas) in the home cluster. All three describe items 
that live in a home, and embeddings reflect this proximity. Boundary 
is function: furniture is structural, home_comfort is textile, 
housewares is utility.

## Common analytics
English name: `furniture_decor`. Large category by SKU count. Revenue 
varies because ticket sizes range from cheap decor to expensive furniture.
